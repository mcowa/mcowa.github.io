Our Mission

  • To act as the sole organization and entity representing the apartment owners of Monte Carlo and to work towards their welfare and interest in a secular manner without any manner of discrimination by caste or religion.
  • To strive for the welfare of the members, being the apartment owners of Monte Carlo and maintaining the properties.
  • To appoint a Facility Management Company, Estate Manager or such professional or other entities or persons as may be deemed fit for carrying out the above activities of MCOWA.
  • To represent and protect the interest of the members of MCOWA as a body of persons/ being the apartment owners, in any litigation, arbitration, negotiations, etc. before. any Government Authorities, Statutory Agencies, Courts, Tribunals, Arbitrations, Builders, Service providers or any third party in matters affecting their interest.
  • To maintain, develop, improve, secure, administer and manage all common amenities including but not limited to water supply; electricity supply (including back-up with diesel generator), cleaning, security arrangements, security systems, CCTVs, automated provisions, water treatment plants, sewage treatment plants, telecom system, dish antenna systems, drainage facility, common area lighting, fire- fighting equipment, rainwater harvesting, and also the personnel for plumbing, electric and other miscellaneous jobs.
  • To address the common and genuine grievances of the members of MCOWA and to redress the same through the Government, Judicial, Statutory and other bodies, including local bodies as may be necessary & applicable.

Note: Our complete mission statement is listed in our Bye-law document.
